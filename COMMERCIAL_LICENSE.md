# PlexMCP Commercial License

## Overview

PlexMCP is available under the Functional Source License (FSL-1.1-Apache-2.0). Organizations with annual revenue exceeding **$1,000,000 USD** require a commercial license for commercial use.

## Who Needs a Commercial License?

You need a commercial license if **both** of the following apply:

1. Your organization's annual revenue exceeds $1,000,000 USD
2. You are using PlexMCP for commercial purposes

## Who Does NOT Need a Commercial License?

- **Self-hosting for internal use** - Organizations of any size can self-host PlexMCP for their own use
- **Organizations under $1M revenue** - Full commercial use permitted
- **Non-commercial use** - Education, research, personal projects
- **Contributing to PlexMCP** - Always welcome regardless of organization size

## Frequently Asked Questions

### How is revenue calculated?

Annual revenue is your organization's total revenue in the most recently completed fiscal year. For subsidiaries, the parent company's total revenue applies.

### What if we grow past $1M during our usage?

Contact us within 30 days of crossing the threshold to arrange a commercial license. We offer reasonable transition periods.

### Can we try before buying?

Yes! You can use PlexMCP under the FSL license for evaluation purposes for up to 90 days, regardless of your organization's size.

### Do we need a license for testing/staging?

No. Non-production environments do not require a commercial license.

### What about open source projects?

Open source projects can use PlexMCP under the FSL license without a commercial license, regardless of their organization's revenue.
